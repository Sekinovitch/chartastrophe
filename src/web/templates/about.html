About Chartastrophe

The Project

Chartastrophe is a humorous project that explores the fascinating world of absurd statistical correlations. Our mission? To demonstrate that correlation does not imply causation, in the most entertaining way possible!

How does it work?

Our sophisticated algorithm:

  1. Collects data from official sources (World Bank, INSEE...)
  2. Analyzes all possible combinations to find correlations
  3. Generates hilarious pseudo-scientific explanations
  4. Presents everything as a parody "scientific study"

Warning

All correlations presented are mathematically correct but deliberately absurd. Do not use them to make important decisions in your life!
